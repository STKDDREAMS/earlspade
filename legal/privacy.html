Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what happens to your information when you visit earlspade or place an order. We've kept it short because, honestly, there isn't much to tell — we don't run our own customer database, marketing trackers, or analytics on this site.

What we collect ourselves

Nothing. earlspade does not operate a server-side database, does not use analytics or tracking scripts, and does not collect, store, or sell any personal information through this website itself.

What Stripe collects

When you place an order, checkout is handled entirely by Stripe, our payment processor. Stripe collects and processes the information needed to complete your purchase, which may include:

  • Your name, email address, and shipping address
  • Payment details (card number, billing address) — these are sent directly to Stripe and never pass through or get stored on our own systems
  • Order details (items purchased, amount, date)

That information is governed by Stripe's own privacy policy, available at stripe.com/privacy. We receive order confirmations and the details needed to fulfill and ship your order, but we don't maintain a separate marketing list or sell that information to anyone.

Cookies

This site does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Stripe's checkout page may use its own cookies as part of fraud prevention and payment processing — that's covered under their privacy policy linked above.

How we use order information

We use the order details Stripe provides us only to fulfill your order — packing and shipping what you bought, and reaching out if there's a problem with it. We don't use it for marketing, and we don't share it with anyone outside of what's required to ship your order (e.g. a shipping carrier).

Your rights

Since we don't hold a separate database of your information, most requests about your personal data (access, correction, deletion) are best directed to Stripe directly, since they're the ones actually storing it. If you have a question about an order itself, reach out to us and we're happy to help.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If anything here changes — for example, if we ever add analytics or a mailing list in the future — we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or an order? Email hello@earlspade.com.
