chapter one — the shore

born in malibu

salt air, long light, slow afternoons. earlspade started twenty steps from the water, and every piece still carries that walk down to the sand. this is not a factory brand — it’s a beach one.

02

chapter two — the series

small, numbered runs

we don’t do endless collections. each series is one small numbered run — a season’s worth, counted piece by piece. when the run sells through, the series closes and moves to the archive.

No. 001 No. 014 No. 048 gone
03

chapter three — the hands

made slow, made small

small batches only. every piece gets picked up, turned over, and checked before it leaves. if we wouldn’t wear it into the water, it doesn’t ship.

04

chapter four — the muse

the sea series

series one belongs to the ocean — the green sea turtle that swims home to the beach where it hatched, the manta, the moorish idol. the muse lives right outside the door.

05

chapter five — the promise

sold, then gone

never mass-made. never re-run. when a series closes it stays closed — the archive is a museum, not a warehouse. owning one means owning one of a counted few.

one run · one season
