earlspade — the archive

Once it’s gone, it’s gone forever.

The Celestial Series was the first thing we ever made — a small, numbered run of pieces cast from star charts we sketched over one summer in Malibu. Every piece was stamped with its number, and the night the last one sold, we broke the molds.

That’s when we decided the archive would be a rule, not an accident. Every earlspade series is a single pressing: one run, one season, and when it closes it moves here and never comes back. No reissues, no restocks, no “back by popular demand.”

You won’t find photos in here, and that’s on purpose. The jewelry is already sold and gone — the only place a Celestial piece exists now is with the person wearing it. If you missed it, you missed it. That’s what makes the ones you catch worth keeping.
